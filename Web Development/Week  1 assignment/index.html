Introduction to Urban Gardening

Urban gardening is the practice of growing plants in an urban environment. Even with limited space, you can enjoy fresh produce, beautiful flowers, and the mental health benefits of gardening.

Key Benefits of Urban Gardening

  • Fresh, organic produce right at your doorstep
  • Improved mental health through connection with nature
  • Reduced carbon footprint from local food production
  • Community building through shared garden spaces
  • Increased biodiversity in urban areas

How to Start Your Urban Garden

  1. Assess your available space (balcony, windowsill, rooftop)
  2. Determine sunlight exposure (most vegetables need 6+ hours)
  3. Choose appropriate containers with drainage
  4. Select high-quality potting soil
  5. Pick plants suited to your space and sunlight
  6. Establish a watering routine
